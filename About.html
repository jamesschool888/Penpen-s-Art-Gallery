Artist Statement

Lovely day! My name is Victor Peñalosa, and I am one of those artists who wants to grow themselves by being together with everyone who has an interest in the arts. I discovered this skill of mine during the pandemic. This skill is the result of thriving and exploring things on my own. That's why I decided to share what I can do by opening a commission, and everyone is free to connect with me and my style, which is vectorizing. If you are looking for this style, feel free to message me. You can do a lot of stuff with my style; you can put it as your wallpaper, on a mug, on a shirt, on everything you want; it's up to you, and I'll do my best as your artist.

I am now accepting commissions again, and all of the necessary questions that might be asked are already answered at each image at the first part of the gallery.
